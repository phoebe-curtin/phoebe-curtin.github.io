Have you ever heard of The Box by Roddy Rich? Yes? Can I tell you a crazy fact.. Everything on the internet is MADE out of boxes! Don’t believe me? Find any website, hold down your left mouse button and drag it all over the screen. See what I see? Boxes!

When beginning to learn CSS, it’s important to understand these boxes and their purpose. Believe it for not, the basic box model actually relies on a series of four boxes, each of which is placed inside the next! Each segment or box has a name and a purpose. Here they are!

* Content - The content of the box, where text and images appear

* Padding - The space between the content, and the border.

* Border - The border that goes around the padding and content.

* Margin - The space outside of the border.

For example, take a look at these Yeezys!

The Yeezys are the CONTENT.

The PADDING is the wrapping.

The BORDER is the shoe box.

The MARGIN is outside of the shoe box.
